Miner's Mystery Math

Welcome to the mine! Press start and you will be given a random number that you will try to match with your crystals. Clicking on a crystal will add a random amount to your score, but you won't know how much the crystal is worth until you click on it. Try to match your score to the target number. If you go over the number, you lose. Each round you play, the value of the random number and the value of the crystals will change.
